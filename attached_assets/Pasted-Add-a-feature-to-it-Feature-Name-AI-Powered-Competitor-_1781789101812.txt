Feature Name: AI-Powered Competitor Website Intelligence & SEO Gap Analysis

Objective:
Allow users to enter their website URL and a competitor's website URL. The system will automatically analyze both websites and generate a detailed competitive intelligence report.

Feature Description:

1. Website Overview Analysis

- Extract website title, meta description, and main content.
- Identify business category and industry.
- Determine company focus and target audience.
- Summarize website purpose.

2. Company Intelligence

- Identify company name.
- Detect products and services offered.
- Extract company description and value proposition.
- Identify unique selling points (USPs).
- Analyze company positioning in the market.

3. Technology Stack Detection

- Detect frontend technologies (React, Angular, Vue, etc.).
- Detect backend technologies (Node.js, Django, PHP, Laravel, etc.).
- Detect CMS platforms (WordPress, Shopify, Wix, etc.).
- Identify analytics and marketing tools.
- Detect hosting and CDN providers when possible.

4. SEO Competitor Analysis

- Compare meta titles and descriptions.
- Compare keyword usage and density.
- Compare heading structure (H1, H2, H3).
- Compare content length and quality.
- Compare readability scores.
- Identify SEO strengths and weaknesses.

5. Content Strategy Analysis

- Identify major content topics.
- Detect blog strategy.
- Identify FAQ sections.
- Analyze content publishing patterns.
- Detect content gaps between websites.

6. Keyword Gap Analysis

- Find keywords competitor ranks for.
- Identify missing keywords in user's website.
- Suggest high-value keywords.
- Recommend keyword opportunities.

7. Product and Service Comparison

- List competitor products and services.
- Compare features and offerings.
- Identify missing offerings in user's website.
- Suggest opportunities for differentiation.

8. User Experience Analysis

- Evaluate navigation structure.
- Analyze mobile responsiveness.
- Detect page speed optimization indicators.
- Review call-to-action placements.
- Analyze customer engagement elements.

9. AI Business Insights

- Summarize competitor strategy.
- Explain what the competitor is doing effectively.
- Identify areas where the user's website is weaker.
- Suggest improvements to outperform competitors.

10. SEO Gap Report
    Generate:

- Overall Competitor Score
- SEO Score Comparison
- Content Score Comparison
- Keyword Gap Report
- Technical SEO Report
- Actionable Recommendations

Expected Output Example:

Company Overview:

- Industry: Digital Marketing
- Products: SEO Tools, Analytics Dashboard
- Target Audience: Marketers and Businesses

Technology Stack:

- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js
- CMS: WordPress

SEO Comparison:
Your Website Score: 72/100
Competitor Score: 89/100

Keyword Opportunities:

- SEO Audit
- Content Optimization
- Technical SEO

Recommendations:

- Improve meta descriptions
- Add FAQ sections
- Increase content length
- Target missing keywords
- Optimize heading structure

Benefits:

- Understand competitor strategy
- Discover SEO opportunities
- Improve content planning
- Increase search visibility
- Gain competitive advantage
